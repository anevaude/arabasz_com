About Me

I'm a Front-End Developer in Boston offering 15+ years of coding expertise. My background is in Graphic Design, so I love working on projects that keep aesthetics, useability and accessibility in mind.

I'm passionate about tech inclusion and started the Boston chapter of Girl Develop It, a national non-profit teaching women how to code. I also enjoy speaking and teaching, having presented at UXPA Boston and Bentley University.

Some of my other interests are beer sampling, collecting absurdly hot sauces and hiking with Starbuck, my awesome Corgi.
