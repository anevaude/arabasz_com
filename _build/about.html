About Me

Over the last 15 years, I've built websites for small startups and global agencies. With a background in Graphic Design, I believe that good design must be understandable, unobtrusive and accessible.

My specialties include design systems and guiding companies in adopting them. I've presented about responsive web design at UXPA and Bentley University.

I'm passionate about tech inclusion and started Girl Develop It in Boston, offering affordable coding classes for women.

Beyond the pixels, you'll find me navigating the fiery world of hot sauces, indulging in craft beers or hiking with my Corgi Starbuck.
